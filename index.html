Biography & Career

Josh Chance (Josh A) was born on September 23, 1996 in the United States. Chance is a Rapper and producer who built an audience as an unsigned artist using platforms like YouTube. This is where he has racked up over 27 million views with songs such as "No Chill" and "Right Now." He also frequentley collaborates with a longtime rap partner Jake Hill.
